Game Theory: Evil gits VS Indignant angles

发表于2025-08-20|更新于2025-08-20
|总字数:352|阅读时长:1分钟|浏览量:

Game Theory is a method of studying strategic situation. 博弈论是一门研究决策处境的学问。

Not strategic situation

  1. Firms working in perfect competition 自由竞争企业
  2. Firms that were monopolists 垄断企业

Game Theory (inclued anyone) can not say that your goals in life should be or whatever.

博弈论乃至任何人不能说出你人生的目标或其他（例如你自己的价值观）

Payoff is an important factor of Game Theory



Let’s play games!

Game 1

$\alpha$ $\beta$
$\alpha$ (B-, B-) (A , C )
$\beta$ (C , A ) (B+, B+)

In this game, your peer and you should choose $\alpha$ or $\beta$.

  1. If you both choose $\alpha$, you will get B-.
  2. If you both choose $\beta$, you will get B+.
  3. If you choose alphabet different, the one choose $\alpha$ get A, and the other get C. （C是个总统分数，笑~）


possible payoff (1) : Evil gits

They only care about their own grades.



Lesson 1   Do not play a strictly dominated strategy.

理由：严格优势策略期望更大。

Lesson 2   Rational choice can be lead to outcome that SUCK

理由：理性下双方都选择优势策略导致结果变差。

Prisoner’s Dilemma 囚徒困境

Game 2

$\alpha$ $\beta$
$\alpha$ (0, 0) (-1, 3)
$\beta$ (-3, 1) (1, 1)

Base in Game 1, we add some penilty(Psychological punishment).

无明显劣势策略，仅改变Payoff，选 $\alpha$ 可以使利益最小范围损失。选 $\beta$ 可能获得最佳。

Coordination Problem 协和谬误

Lesson 3   You can’t get what you want, till you know what you want. 汝欲求之，必先知之。

Lesson 4   Put yourself in others’ shoes and try to figure out what they will do. 换位思考
